  1. Pixel Game is a simple game in which you try to guess the image with the least amount of flips.

  2. Time is key

    Every 5 minutes you earn 2 flips for you flip bank.

  3. There is a limit

    However, your flip bank can only hold 12 flips.

  4. Guess

    All solutions are one word answers. However the word may be plural. For example apple and apples could be two different solutions, but red apples can not.

  5. Have Fun!

    Lookout for bonuses that can help you figure out the solution quicker.
